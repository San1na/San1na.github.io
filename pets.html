📈 Pet Size & Type Scaling Math

A pet's base ability is multiplied by its Size and its Type (e.g. Huge Rainbow).

Total Boost = Size Multiplier × Type Multiplier

  • Sizes: Normal (1x), Big (2x), Huge (3x)
  • Types: Normal (1x), Rainbow (1.25x)

Example: A Normal Bunny gives +5 Walk Speed. A Huge Rainbow Bunny gives: 5 × (3 × 1.25) = +18.75 Walk Speed!

🦝 The Raccoon Exception

The Super Raccoon's "Steal Limit" ability does NOT use normal multiplication. It uses hardcoded flats:

  • Normal Raccoon: +25 limit
  • Big Raccoon: +50 limit
  • Huge Raccoon: +100 limit

If the Raccoon is Rainbow, it simply adds a flat +10 to the total (e.g. Huge Rainbow = 110).
