⚠️ Do Sprinklers Stack?

NO! According to the game's hidden GrowthBoostSources code, all Sprinklers share the same boost ID category. This means if you place 5 Super Sprinklers around one plant, the game will NOT multiply their stats. It simply picks the single highest buff affecting that plant.
