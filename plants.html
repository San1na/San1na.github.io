Size Multiplier System

Plants have hidden Luck Tiers that exponentially multiply their weight.

  • Base Tier: 0.95x - 1.05x
  • Tier 4 (Epic): 3.8x - 4.2x
  • Tier 6 (Mythic): 9.5x - 12.5x
  • Tier 8 (Godly): 20.0x - 35.0x

Note: After the base size is picked, the server has a hidden chance (1% to 5%) to repeatedly double the final weight up to 10,000,000.

Fruit Sell Value Formula

The exact formula used to calculate how much a fruit sells for:

Final = Base * WeightMut * MutationMut * WeatherMut * FriendMut

  • Weight Penalty: Weight scales nicely until 5kg. After 5kg, a diminishing returns penalty applies to the math so you don't get infinite money.
  • Single Harvest Penalty: Plants that die after one harvest get heavily nerfed mutation multipliers to balance the economy.
  • Friend Boost: +10% sell value for every friend on your plot!
  • Weather Damage: Bad weather can reduce the sell price of fruit by up to 80% (leaving you with a 0.2x multiplier).
Weather Mechanics
  • 🌧️ Rain: Grants 2x Growth Speed (300s).
  • ⚡ Lightning: Chance for Electric fruit (300s).
  • 🌈 Rainbow: Boosts Rainbow luck (300s).
  • ❄️ Snowfall: Chance for Frozen fruit (150s).
  • ☄️ Starfall: Chance for Starstruck fruit (150s).
Offline Growth Boost

When you leave the game, your farm calculates Overtime Growth multipliers when you return:

  • 3 hours: 1.5x Multiplier
  • 24 hours: 2.5x Multiplier
  • 200 hours: 3.5x Multiplier
  • 2000 hours: 5.0x Multiplier (Max hard cap, scales logarithmically after)
